What Is It?

PostAI: A social media helper.

  • PostAI is a tool that uses Machine Learning to help companies and users produce engaging and effective social media posts.
  • With a few parameters and a prompt PostAI generates effective tweets and blog posts.

What Can It Do?

Current capabilities consist of:

  • Generate Tweets and Blog posts
  • Search the web for up to date info on the prompt topic
  • Edit the Tweet or Blog directly in PostAI
  • Share buttons:
    • Post to Twitter to bring you directly to Twitter for quick posting
    • Copy to clipboard so you can post anywhere
    • Email the post contents to yourself or others
